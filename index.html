Samidha: The Learning Hub

Samidha is an organisation aiming towards the better future of society and people by providing academic education and soft skills training to children. Providing education to children who are underprevilaged has been the the goal since Samidha's begining. Established in 2013 by a group of young and enthusiastic college students as a society in G.B. Pant Govt. Engineering College. We welcome you to explore more about the NPO.
